# Aesthetic Interests

What attracts — the tastes in art, literature, music, design, and expression style.

## Core Aesthetic Preferences

### 1. Structural sense and depth come first

What truly attracts is work with structural sense, depth of thought, and internal logic. Whether text, image, or system of thought, structure and depth matter more than surface technique.

### 2. Preference for complexity and authenticity

Keep distance from simplistic narratives. Prefer expression that accommodates complexity, does not回避 contradictions, and does not offer cheap answers.

### 3. Cultivated taste > passing trends

Aesthetic preferences are not driven by trends but formed through long-term exposure and comparison into stable orientation.

### 4. Different domains, different emphasis

- **Writing**: logical rigor, high information density, clear structure
- **Systems of thought**: explanatory power, ability to accommodate counterexamples
- **Design/visual**: function over decoration, simplicity over繁复
- **Music/sound**: layered structure and depth

## Aesthetic and Creation

Aesthetic preferences directly influence creative style. Output tends toward:
- No cheap conclusions
- Preserving complexity of argumentation
- Clear structure and层次
- Abstract judgments supported by concrete examples

## Tensions

### 1. Aesthetic ideal vs current creative ability

Aesthetic standards exceeding current skill level is normal. Continuous practice is more effective than waiting for inspiration.

### 2. Niche taste vs communication needs

Do not fully cater to the mass, nor deliberately追求 niche. Find clarity of expression while maintaining personal style.
