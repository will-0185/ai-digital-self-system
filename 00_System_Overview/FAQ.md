# FAQ

## Is this a prompt collection?

No. Prompts are only one part of the system. The core is the filesystem architecture and long-term evolution mechanism.

## Is this a chatbot persona?

No. A digital self is not a roleplay persona. It is a structured representation of thinking, judgment, knowledge, creation, and evolution.

## Do I need a specific AI model?

No. The system is model-agnostic. It can be used with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, local models, or future AI systems.

## Should I put my private files on GitHub?

Not in a public repository. Use local storage or a private repository for private materials.

## How do I start?

Read `AI_Digital_Self_System.md`, copy the structure to your local filesystem, write your own kernel, and start recording.
