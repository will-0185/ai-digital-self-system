# External Knowledge Archive

A single-file archive of external knowledge, organized by topic. Each entry includes links, excerpts, and AI summaries.

This is a public-safe template. Replace with your own archive entries.

## Format

```markdown
### Topic Name

- Source: [title](url)
- Excerpt: ...
- Summary: ...
- Personal relevance: ...
```

## Note

Do not include full copyrighted materials. Keep excerpts short and link to originals.
